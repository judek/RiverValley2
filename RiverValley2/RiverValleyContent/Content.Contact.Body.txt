--+++ Thanks for visiting us!
We define the church as the universal body of believers who follow Jesus Christ, rather than an institution, building, or specific group of people. We are committed to building a growing community of followers of Jesus Christ through Life Groups, small groups for men, women, and children who share a desire to grow together spiritually. Life Groups meet weekly in the homes of group members, in the manner of the Acts 2 church. We come together each Sunday for a family reunion and celebration of the resurrection of Jesus Christ.
